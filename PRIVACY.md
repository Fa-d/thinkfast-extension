# Intently - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 8, 2026
**Last Updated**: January 8, 2026

## Overview

Intently is a browser extension designed to help you build healthier browsing habits through mindful interventions and usage tracking. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Local Storage (Always Active)

The following data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API:

- Website URLs you choose to track
- Time spent on tracked websites
- Daily usage statistics and goals
- Extension settings and preferences
- Session history and intervention interactions
- Current streaks and achievement data

This data **never leaves your device** unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

### Cloud Sync (Optional - User Must Enable)

If you choose to sign in with Google for cloud sync:

- **Email address**: Used for authentication only
- **Usage data**: Your goals, statistics, and settings are encrypted and synced to Supabase
- **Authentication tokens**: Securely stored to maintain your session

**Cloud sync is completely optional.** The extension works fully offline without signing in.

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Data Usage

- Track your progress toward browsing goals
- Display personalized usage statistics and insights
- Determine optimal timing for showing interventions
- Save your preferences and settings
- Calculate streaks and achievements

### Cloud Sync Data (If Enabled)

- Backup your data securely
- Sync across multiple devices
- Restore data if you reinstall the extension
- Enable cross-device goal tracking

## Permissions Explained

### Required Permissions

- **storage**: Save your goals and statistics locally on your device
- **tabs**: Monitor active websites to track time on sites you choose
- **alarms**: Schedule periodic checks for accurate time tracking
- **idle**: Detect when you're away to pause tracking (accurate statistics)

### Optional Permissions (User Consent Required)

- **identity**: Enable Google Sign-In for cloud sync (only if you choose)
- **notifications**: Show optional reminders (can be disabled in settings)

### Advanced Permissions

- **activeTab**: Detect when you visit a tracked website
- **contextMenus**: Right-click menu to snooze interventions
- **host permissions (<all_urls>)**: Display intervention overlays on tracked sites only

**Note**: Host permissions are required to show intervention screens on websites you choose to track. We only monitor and interact with user-configured websites.
